WELCOME TO THE CODING QUIZ!

You'll answer the multiple choice 10 question quiz.

You'll have 10 seconds per question.

For each correct answer, you'll move to the next question with no penalties.

For each incorrect question, you'll lose 5 seconds on the timer. Your goal is to get a final score of 0 or more.

At the end, you'll be able to enter your name into the Highscore board.

Have fun!
